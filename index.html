About pinkJeans

Founded in 2020, pinkJeans is a premium women's fashion brand dedicated to creating stylish, comfortable clothing that empowers women to look and feel their best.

Our mission is to provide high-quality, fashionable pieces that are designed to last. We believe that every woman deserves to feel confident in what she wears, which is why we focus on flattering fits, premium fabrics, and timeless designs with modern touches.

We're passionate about sustainable fashion and ethical manufacturing. All our products are made in facilities that ensure fair working conditions, and we're continuously working to reduce our environmental impact.
